About Me

A developer who also keeps the systems running

I sit at the intersection of software engineering and IT operations — shipping web platforms while making sure the infrastructure behind them stays healthy.

I'm a results-driven Web Developer and IT Support Specialist with hands-on experience building, maintaining, and supporting web applications using Laravel and WordPress, while managing IT infrastructure in a demanding academic environment.

Day to day, I support institutional portals, Computer-Based Test (CBT) systems, web platforms, hardware troubleshooting, system administration, and end-user support — bringing both engineering rigor and practical operations experience to every project.

Whether you're a startup needing a polished web product, an organization that needs reliable IT operations, or a team looking for a remote developer who can ship and support — I deliver work that's clean, dependable, and built to last.
